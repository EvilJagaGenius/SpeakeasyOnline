5 - THE GUNMAN
Like a Thug, but meaner, louder, and packing Chicago Typewriters.

MASSACRE: Tap the Gunman and remove a card from the game.  You lose 1 Reputation, the target player gains 1 Info.
ARMED ROBBERY: Tap a Gunman and any number of Thugs, gain $2 for every card tapped.
ASSASSINATE: Tap a Burglar alongside the Gunman and spend 1 Info.  Remove a card from the game.
