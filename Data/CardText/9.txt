9 - THE BURGLAR
Muscle and gunmen are well and good, but some jobs take a little more... finesse.

ROB: Tap the Burglar and spend X Info.  Steal $X from another player.
BOOZE HEIST: Tap the Smuggler alongside the Burglar and spend X Info.  Steal X Booze from another player.
HIDE EVIDENCE: Tap the Burglar; adjust a player's Info by 1.
