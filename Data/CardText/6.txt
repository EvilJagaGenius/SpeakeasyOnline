6 - THE BARKEEPER
A good cocktail keeps the customers coming back for more, and loosens their tongues for dropping useful information.

TIPS: Tap the Barkeeper and spend X Booze.  Shuffle X cards back into the deck and gain X money.
"TIPS": Tap the Barkeeper.  Spend 1 Booze, gain 1 Info.
MIX DRINKS: Tap the Barkeeper and spend X Booze to put X cards into your Customer pile.
