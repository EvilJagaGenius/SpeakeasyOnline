A - THE RAT
The vilest, scummiest individual in the deck, the Rat is a double-agent who can sabotage other gangster's plans.  Useful... but never trusted.

DOUBLE-CROSS: Tap the Rat and another card in response to another player using that card's action.  Cancel the other player's action, you may use that card's action if you wish.
HAD IT COMING: Arresting the Rat costs 1 less Info.  Massacring the Rat costs 1 less Reputation.
